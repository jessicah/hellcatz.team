The Hellcatz is the ladies only sub-team of the Bolt Race Team. We’re a passionate, supportive group of women with representation in all grades, A—D, and from all over the world. With the aid of Velocity Vixen and ZSUN, the Hellcatz created the wildly popular Wild Women Racing Series, which has seen over 200 women competing in our first series.

Our team is built on the principles of passion, trust, courage, positivity and friendship.

To be a part of the wickedly awesome Hellcatz, please complete our membership form. We’ll send you an e-mail with details about joining the team on ZwiftPower, links to our Facebook groups, and an invite to Discord.
